Welcome to TAR WORLD Restaurant

A World of Flavors!

Indulge your taste buds in an extraordinary culinary journey that spans the globe right here at TAR WORLD. We are more than just a restaurant; we are a fusion of cultures, a celebration of diversity, and a place where culinary artistry knows no boundaries.

Western

From hearty burgers and crispy fries to perfectly grilled steaks, we offer a mouthwatering selection of Western favorites that will satisfy your cravings.

Chinese

Indulge in a diverse range of dishes that represent the culinary diversity of China. From savory stir-fries to delicate dim sum, we offer a true taste of this fascinating cuisine.

Japanese

we celebrate the beauty of Japanese cuisine. From delicate sushi and sashimi to hearty ramen and flavorful teriyaki, our menu is a tribute to the diverse and harmonious flavors of Japan.

Islam

we take pride in crafting dishes that bring together the culinary traditions of various Islamic regions. From fragrant nasi lemak to savory nasi kandar, our menu reflects the rich tapestry of flavors.
